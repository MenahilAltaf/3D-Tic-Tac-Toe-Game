ABOUT THE GAME

A familiar game, rebuilt in three dimensions.

TRIAD keeps the elegant logic of tic-tac-toe but gives it real depth — literally. Every tile lives on a rendered 3D board you can orbit and zoom, every move drops a glowing piece into place, and every win draws a beam of light across the field you claimed.

Built entirely on Three.js with a hand-written camera rig and an unbeatable minimax opponent, it's designed to feel like a premium puzzle cabinet — not a browser demo.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need to install anything to play?+

No. TRIAD runs entirely in your browser using WebGL through Three.js. Just open the Play Game page.

Is my progress saved?+

Yes — your score, move history, and leaderboard entries are saved locally in your browser using Local Storage.

Can I play against a friend instead of the AI?+

Yes. Switch to "2 Players" mode on the Play Game page to pass control back and forth on the same device.

Is the AI actually unbeatable?+

On Hard difficulty, yes — it uses a full minimax search, so the best outcome against it is a draw. Easy mode plays more casually.

Is there a physical version of the game?+

Yes, see the Buy Game page for the Standard and Collector's editions, including pricing and what's included.
