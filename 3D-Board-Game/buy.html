STORE FAQ

Before you buy.

How long does shipping take?+

Standard and Collector's editions ship within 5–7 business days domestically, with international delivery typically taking 10–14 days.

Does the physical board need batteries?+

Only the Collector's edition, for its LED win-line effect. It ships with a rechargeable battery and USB-C cable included.

Can I upgrade from Standard to Collector's later?+

There's currently no upgrade path between physical editions, but your digital account and leaderboard history carry over regardless of which one you own.

Is there a return policy?+

Yes — unused, unopened editions can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
